# Download Ready-Made DOCX Files for Testing

## Free Sample DOCX Files

### Option 1: Microsoft Templates
https://templates.office.com/
- Download any report or document template
- They usually have mixed formatting
- Perfect for testing!

### Option 2: Sample Files Repository
https://file-examples.com/index.php/sample-documents-download/sample-doc-download/
- Free sample DOCX files
- Various sizes and formats

### Option 3: Create from Template Sites
https://www.template.net/business/word-templates/
- Professional document templates
- Download as DOCX

### Option 4: University Sample Documents
Search Google for: "sample academic paper docx"
- Many universities provide sample formatted papers
- These often have strict formatting rules (perfect for testing!)

## What to Look For

Good test documents should have:
- ✅ Multiple heading levels
- ✅ Several paragraphs of body text
- ✅ Variety of font styles
- ✅ Different alignments
- ✅ At least 2-3 pages

## After Downloading

1. Open the DOCX file
2. Look at what fonts/sizes are used
3. Set your app rules to match (or intentionally mismatch)
4. Upload and see the results!

## Example Test Scenario

**Download a template with:**
- Headings in Calibri 18pt
- Body in Calibri 11pt

**Set your app rules to:**
- Heading Font: Times New Roman (will find errors!)
- Heading Size: 16 (will find errors!)
- Body Font: Arial (will find errors!)
- Body Size: 12 (will find errors!)

**Result:** The app should detect ALL the mismatches!
